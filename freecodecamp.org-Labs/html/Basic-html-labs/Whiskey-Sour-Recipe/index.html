Cocktail Recipe: Whiskey Sour

Spirit, citrus, and sugar are the base ingredients mixed to create a classical sour. One of such drinks is the Whiskey Sour. The Whiskey Sour is a classic drink with recipes dating back to the 1800s. Generally speaking, the Whiskey Sour is traditionally made with 4 ingredients: whiskey, lemon juice, sugar, and egg white. (The egg white helps tame some of the tart flavor and gives the cocktail a richer and smoother texture.)

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • 1 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1 oz Simple Syrup
  • Optional: .75 oz Egg White

Instructions

  1. In a shaker, mixed the ingredients and dry shake for 20-30 seconds.
  2. Add ice and shake for another 15-20 seconds or until chilled.
  3. Double strain into a rocks glass over ice.
  4. Optional: Garnish with 3-4 drops of angustura bitters.
